IDEAL FORMAT for each account card.
Each card = 3 short lines. 8–12 words per line.

Example:

  Walmart Inc. · Retail · Bentonville, AR · Priority 9 (High likelihood to engage)
  🚨 ORC rings coordinating across 3 regional districts
  ⏰ Regional leads can't correlate, corporate security blind
  🎯 5,000 stores need centralized incident correlation now

Another example:

  Toyota Manufacturing · Automotive · Plano, TX · Priority 9 (High likelihood to engage)
  🚨 Workplace violence incident at Kentucky plant, 60+ sites in dark
  ⏰ Plant leaders demanding real-time safety visibility across all facilities
  🎯 Distributed security teams need unified threat feed immediately

NOT like this:

  🚨 Toyota's organized retail crime correlation workflows are currently facing challenges because the data ingestion pipelines aren't unified, which means that... (TOO LONG, TECHNICAL)

Rules:
- Each line is one short concrete statement
- Name real facts (dollar amounts, site counts, city names)
- No hedging words
- No paragraphs
