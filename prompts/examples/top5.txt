IDEAL FORMAT for the Top-5 priority section.
Each account = ONE line. Format: Company — Trigger → Business impact.
Maximally punchy. No filler. Name a real fact or dollar amount.

Examples:

1. Walmart — ORC rings across Midwest → $60B losses accelerating
2. Target — $100M loss prevention under exec scrutiny → ROI due Q2
3. Ford — Workplace threat lockdown at Ohio plant → 60+ plants blind
4. J&J — Vendor fraud across 3 operating companies → Compliance demanding unity
5. GE — Social engineering across divisions → CISO ordered intel sharing

NOT like this (too wordy, too soft, too analytical):

1. Walmart announced Q4 inventory shrink reports and... (TOO LONG)
2. Target may want to consider their loss prevention... (TOO SOFT)
3. Corporate security at Ford is currently dealing with... (TOO VAGUE)

Rules:
- Drop filler: "Corporate", "Currently", "The team", "is now"
- Verbs > nouns. Active voice only.
- Company name — trigger (punchy) → outcome (punchy)
- Each side of the arrow should fit naturally in 5–7 words
